# Typography & Motion

## 1. Font Pairings
To achieve a professional yet modern feel, we use three distinct font families:

| Purpose | Category | Recommendation | Style |
|---------|----------|----------------|-------|
| **Headers** | Serif | *Playfair Display* or *Instrument Serif* | Professional, elegant, authoritative. |
| **Body** | Rounded Sans | *Geist* or *Inter* (Rounded) | Friendly, modern, highly readable. |
| **Data/Logs** | Monospace | *JetBrains Mono* or *Fira Code* | Precise, tech-focused, clear character distinction. |

## 2. Type Scale
- **Display (H1):** Serif, 2.5rem, Semi-bold, tight letter spacing.
- **Heading (H2/H3):** Serif, 1.5rem - 1.25rem, Medium.
- **Body:** Rounded Sans, 0.875rem (default), 1.25 line height.
- **Monospace:** 0.75rem - 0.8125rem, consistent line height for log alignment.

## 3. Motion & Transitions
- **Duration:** 200ms - 300ms for layout changes.
- **Easing:** `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` (standard ease-in-out).
- **Panel Resizing:** Smooth width/height transitions; avoid jittery content reflow.
- **Hover States:** Subtle lift (y-axis) or glow effect on glass cards.
